1. NovaSpark Pro X Laptop
Product Description
The NovaSpark Pro X is TechMart's flagship laptop, engineered for professionals who demand
uncompromising performance. Powered by the latest 13th-generation Intel Core i9 processor paired
with 32 GB of LPDDR5 RAM, it handles everything from large-scale data science workloads to
high-resolution video editing without breaking a sweat. The 15.6-inch OLED display delivers true
blacks, a 120 Hz refresh rate, and 100% DCI-P3 colour coverage, making it a favourite among
creative professionals.
Storage is handled by a blazing-fast 2 TB NVMe SSD that reaches sequential read speeds of 7,000
MB/s, ensuring near-instant application launches and file transfers. The dedicated NVIDIA GeForce
RTX 4070 GPU with 8 GB GDDR6 VRAM enables real-time ray tracing and AI-assisted rendering.
Despite its power, the chassis remains slim at just 17.8 mm thick and weighs 1.92 kg thanks to a
premium CNC-machined aluminium body.
Connectivity options include Thunderbolt 4, USB-A 3.2, HDMI 2.1, a full-size SD card reader, and
Wi-Fi 6E with Bluetooth 5.3. The 86 Wh battery supports up to 14 hours of mixed usage, and the
included 140 W USB-C GaN charger can refill the battery to 50% in just 30 minutes. The NovaSpark
Pro X ships with Windows 11 Pro and a two-year on-site warranty.

4. ClearAir Pro Air Purifier
Product Description
The ClearAir Pro is a smart air purifier built for medium to large rooms of up to 60 square metres. Its
four-stage filtration system — comprising a pre-filter, True HEPA H13 filter, activated carbon layer,
and UV-C sterilisation stage — removes 99.97% of particles as small as 0.3 microns, including dust,
pollen, pet dander, mould spores, and airborne bacteria.
A built-in air quality sensor continuously monitors PM2.5, CO2, humidity, and temperature,
automatically adjusting fan speed to maintain optimal air quality. The companion mobile app provides
real-time air quality readings, filter life tracking, scheduling, and historical data charts. With a noise
level as low as 22 dB in sleep mode, the ClearAir Pro operates silently throughout the night.

6. VitaSense Blood Glucose Meter
Product Description
The VitaSense is a clinically validated blood glucose monitoring device designed for people
managing diabetes at home. Using electrochemical biosensor technology, it delivers accurate
readings within 5 seconds using a blood sample as small as 0.5 microlitres. The measuring range
spans from 1.1 to 33.3 mmol/L (20–600 mg/dL), covering both hypoglycaemic and hyperglycaemic
states.
Readings are automatically synced to the VitaSense Health app via Bluetooth, where users can track
trends, set glucose target ranges, log meals and medication, and share reports with healthcare
providers as PDF exports. The device stores up to 500 readings on-board and runs on a standard
CR2032 battery lasting approximately 12 months. Each kit includes the meter, 50 test strips, 50
lancets, a lancing device, a carrying case, and a quick-start guide.
